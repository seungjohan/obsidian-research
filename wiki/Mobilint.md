# Mobilint

Mobilint is a fabless AI semiconductor company specializing in high-performance, low-power NPUs for edge computing and industrial automation.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Semiconductors / Edge AI / Industrial NPU.
- **Mission:** To overcome GPU limitations by delivering affordable, efficient, "server-class" inference at the edge.
- **Vision:** "AI Starts Here" (Powering ubiquitous on-device and on-premise AI).
- **Status:** Series C Scale-up (Global presence; ISSCC 2026 presenter).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **POSCO DX Partnership:** Developing NPU-based solutions for industrial logistics, robotics, and manufacturing to replace GPU stacks.
- **ARES & REGULUS:** Commercialized 80 TOPS NPUs and next-gen SoCs that won CES Innovation Awards (2025/2026).
- **MLX-A1 Edge AI Box:** Launched a scalable "AI Box" capable of 320 TOPS for high-end edge servers.
- **On-Device LLM:** Optimized chips to run 8B-14B parameter models locally with sub-second latency.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Funding:** Successfully closed a ₩70B (~$51M) Series C in April 2026.
- **Efficiency:** ARIES chip operates at ~25W (vs. hundreds of watts for GPUs).
- **Recognition:** Highlighted at ISSCC 2026 alongside Nvidia and Microsoft.
- **Scalability:** Architecture supports 400+ deep learning models including Transformers and VLMs.
