# VoyagerX

VoyagerX is an AI-driven productivity startup famous for **Vrew**, a text-based video editor that revolutionized content creation for the prosumer market.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Software / Creator Economy / SaaS.
- **Mission:** "Edit less, create more."
- **Vision:** To empower everyone to become a creator by removing technical barriers through human-centric AI.
- **Status:** High-growth Scale-up (Unicorn Candidate).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Vrew 3.0:** Transitioned to an All-in-One AI Production Hub featuring generative script-to-video, voice cloning, and AI speech correction.
- **Enterprise Pivot:** Expanding from YouTubers to corporate training, marketing teams, and education sectors.
- **Global Scaling:** Strong growth in Japan, North America, and Southeast Asia.
- **Human-AI Collaboration:** Focus on AI as a "collaborator" that automates the non-creative tasks (silence removal, subtitling).

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **User Base:** 5 million+ global users.
- **Business Model:** SaaS with AI Credit-based usage for generative features.
- **Platform Reach:** Multi-platform (Windows, Mac, Linux, Mobile).
- **Reputation:** Rated "Highest Relevance" for Creator UX in the 2026 Korean AI landscape.
