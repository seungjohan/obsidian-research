# AI Industry Map 2026: The Triple Intersection

In 2026, the AI industry is defined by three primary pillars. The "cutting edge" lies where these pillars overlap.

## 🏛️ The Three Pillars

### 1. Agentic AI (The "Digital Brain")
AI that doesn't just respond, but **executes outcomes**. It uses multi-agent systems to plan, reason, and use tools autonomously.
- **Key Trend:** Shift from "Copilots" to "Silicon Employees."
- **Representative Companies:** [[Upstage]], [[Allganize]], [[Scatter-Lab]] (Zeta).

### 2. Physical AI (The "Embodied Body")
AI that interacts with the **three-dimensional world**. It requires spatial intelligence and "World Models" to understand physics.
- **Key Trend:** Simulation-to-Reality (Sim2Real) and Software-Defined Robotics (SDR).
- **Representative Companies:** [[Bear-Robotics]], [[DeepX]], [[Mobilint]].

### 3. Vertical AI (The "Expert Soul")
AI trained on **proprietary, domain-specific data**. It captures labor budgets rather than just IT budgets.
- **Key Trend:** Regulatory-native models for Healthcare, Legal, and Industry.
- **Representative Companies:** [[Lunit]] (Healthcare), [[MakinaRocks]] (Industrial), [[MarqVision]] (IP/Legal).

## 🔗 The Intersections: Connecting the Dots

### A. Physical + Vertical = "Autonomous Industry"
*Where specialized domain knowledge meets physical execution.*
- **The Concept:** Robots that don't just move, but understand the specific engineering or biological context of their environment.
- **Intersection Point:** [[MakinaRocks]] + [[Bear-Robotics]].
- **Company Dots:**
    - **[[Bear-Robotics]]**: Using its `--model` flag to adapt its "brain" to specific industrial bodies (Carti/Cardi).
    - **[[MakinaRocks]]**: Providing the anomaly detection and "industrial soul" to physical machines.
    - **[[AIMMO]]**: Providing the high-quality data operations for autonomous physical movement.

### B. Agentic + Vertical = "Autonomous Expert"
*Where digital agents become specialized knowledge workers.*
- **The Concept:** Agents that can handle end-to-end workflows in regulated industries without human hand-holding.
- **Intersection Point:** [[Lunit]] + [[Upstage]].
- **Company Dots:**
    - **[[Lunit]]**: Moving from "AI Diagnostics" to "Agentic Oncology" (planning treatment paths).
    - **[[Allganize]]**: Enabling enterprises to build their own agentic workflows using sLLMs.
    - **[[Sendbird]]**: Transforming customer communication into autonomous, verticalized support agents.

### C. Agentic + Physical = "Cyber-Physical Organisms"
*Where digital brains control fleets of physical bodies.*
- **The Concept:** A centralized "agentic" brain that orchestrates hundreds of physical robots as a single workforce.
- **Intersection Point:** [[Twelve-Labs]] + [[Bear-Robotics]].
- **Company Dots:**
    - **[[Twelve-Labs]]**: Providing the "Video Understanding" that allows agents to "see" and interpret the physical world.
    - **[[Bear-Robotics]]**: The "Bear Universe" system that coordinates 500+ robots autonomously.
    - **[[DeepBrain-AI]]**: Connecting agentic brains with physical-feeling "AI Humans" for service.

## 🎯 The "Triple Intersection" (The 2026 Frontier)
The ultimate goal: **Self-Healing, Self-Managing Ecosystems.**
- **Scenario:** A factory where **Vertical AI** (MakinaRocks) detects a fault, **Agentic AI** (Upstage) plans the repair and orders parts, and **Physical AI** (Bear Robotics/Humanoids) executes the repair.
