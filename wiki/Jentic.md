# Jentic

Jentic is a Dublin-based startup that provides the infrastructure for building autonomous agent systems. Their platform allows developers to define complex goals and then automatically orchestrates the necessary AI agents and tool-calls to achieve them.

## 🧠 Agentic Orchestration
- **Workflow Automation:** Moving from linear scripts to goal-based autonomous execution.
- **Tool Integration:** Easy connection to APIs, databases, and enterprise software.
- **State Management:** Keeping track of an agent's reasoning and progress across long-running tasks.
