# Akara Robotics

Akara Robotics is a Dublin-based leader in healthcare robotics. Spun out of Trinity College Dublin, the company designs AI-powered robots like *Violet* to autonomously disinfect clinical environments using UV-C light and advanced navigation.

## 🚀 Strategic Overview
- **KPMG Global Tech Innovator (2025):** Recognized as Ireland's top tech innovator for their impact on healthcare efficiency.
- **Vertical Focus:** Unlike general-purpose AMRs, Akara's robots are specialized for hospital wards, where infection control and interaction with clinical staff are paramount.
- **Physical AI Implementation:** Uses proprietary SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) optimized for messy, high-traffic hospital environments.

## 🛠️ Key Milestones (2025–2026)
- **2025:** Deployed *Violet* robots across major NHS (UK) and HSE (Ireland) hospitals.
- **2026:** Launched *Akara Care*, a next-generation social robot designed to assist in elderly care facilities with basic tasks and monitoring.
- **International Expansion:** Secured major hospital contracts in Germany and the US.
