# FriendliAI

FriendliAI is a frontier AI inference platform that optimizes the deployment and serving of large language models (LLMs) through its proprietary PeriFlow engine.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Infrastructure / Cloud / LLM Serving.
- **Mission:** To make high-performance AI inference accessible, scalable, and cost-efficient for enterprises.
- **Vision:** To democratize access to production-grade AI.
- **Status:** Series A Scale-up (Led by the pioneer of Continuous Batching).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **PeriFlow Engine:** Delivering industry-leading throughput and latency via speculative decoding and custom GPU kernels.
- **Samsung Cloud Alliance:** Partnering with SCP to provide optimized inference on NVIDIA B300 GPUs.
- **InferenceSense™:** Launched a tool for enterprises to manage and monetize idle GPU capacity.
- **Global Leadership:** Hired Brian Yoo (ex-Moloco COO) to lead global hypergrowth.
- **Regulated Sectors:** Achieved SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA compliance for Healthcare and Finance clients.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Growth:** Revenue grew 6x-7x in 2025; now powers 544,000+ models via Hugging Face.
- **Efficiency:** Typically 2x+ faster throughput than standard vLLM or TGI setups.
- **Client Base:** Includes LG Electronics (EXAONE), SKT, and Upstage.
- **Funding:** ~$27M cumulative.
