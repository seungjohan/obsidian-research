# 11xAI

11xAI is a London-based startup at the forefront of the **Agentic AI** revolution. Their flagship product, Alice, is an autonomous Sales Development Representative (SDR) that can research leads, craft personalized outreach, and manage follow-ups without human intervention.

## 🤖 The "Digital Worker" Model
- **Outcome-Oriented:** Focused on booked meetings, not just sent emails.
- **Multi-Channel:** Operates across email, LinkedIn, and other social platforms.
- **Continuous Learning:** Updates its strategy based on response rates and conversion data.
