# Rezolve AI

Rezolve AI is a Dublin-based company that provides an AI-powered Service Desk. It integrates with platforms like Microsoft Teams and Slack to provide employees with instant, autonomous resolution to their IT and HR queries.

## 🤖 Service Automation
- **GenAI Native:** Built on Large Language Models to understand complex, natural language requests.
- **Autonomous Resolution:** Capable of performing tasks like resetting passwords, granting access, and software installation without human help.
- **KB Integration:** Automatically digests enterprise knowledge bases to provide accurate, context-aware answers.
