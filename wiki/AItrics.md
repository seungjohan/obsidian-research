# AItrics

AItrics is a medical AI company specializing in real-time patient monitoring and crisis prediction through its VitalCare platform.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Healthcare AI / Medical Software.
- **Mission:** To transform reactive disease care into predictive, preventive, and personalized care.
- **Vision:** "AI that saves lives" (Reliable machine learning for high-stakes healthcare).
- **Status:** Series C Scale-up ($50M+ cumulative funding).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Global Regulatory Success:** Secured medical device approval in Indonesia and MDSAP certification for US, Canada, Japan, and Australia.
- **VitalCare (VC):** Real-time EMR monitoring that predicts cardiac arrest (24h), sepsis (4h), and death (6h) with high accuracy.
- **V.Doc Pro:** Launched an AI clinical copilot using LLMs to automate pre-consultation and clinical documentation.
- **Mayo Clinic Partnership:** Collaborating on model validation for the US market.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Funding:** Raised ₩35 Billion ($24M) in Series C (late 2025/2026).
- **Investors:** Premier Partners, Mirae Asset Capital, KB Securities.
- **Social Impact:** Reducing "alarm fatigue" and ICU transfers while improving patient outcomes.
- **Market Scope:** Expanding into Southeast Asia and the US to democratize predictive intelligence.
