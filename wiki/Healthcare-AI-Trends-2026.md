# Healthcare AI Trends (2026)

The 2026 medical AI landscape is defined by the transition from "R&D curiosity" to "Standard of Care."

## 🏥 Key Sectors
- **Precision Oncology:** Using AI to predict biomarkers and drug responses ([[Lunit]], [[Coredotbio]]).
- **Biosignal Prediction:** Real-time monitoring for cardiac arrest and sepsis ([[VUNO]], [[AItrics]]).
- **Retinal Biomarkers:** Non-invasive systemic health dashboards ([[MediWhale]]).
- **AI Drug Discovery:** Reducing lead optimization from years to months ([[Standigm]]).

## 🚀 Commercial Shifts
- **The Profitability Pivot:** Companies are moving away from venture-dependency toward self-sustainability (e.g., Lunit's 2026 break-even goal).
- **Consolidation:** Strategic acquisitions like Lunit's integration of Volpara Health are creating global medical AI giants.
