# Gradient Labs

Gradient Labs is a London-based startup focused on creating autonomous AI agents for customer operations, with a particular emphasis on the Fintech sector. Founded by early Monzo employees, they understand the balance between automation and regulatory compliance.

## 💳 Fintech Focus
- **Complex Query Resolution:** Going beyond simple FAQs to handle account-specific issues.
- **Regulatory Guardrails:** Ensuring AI responses adhere to financial services regulations.
- **Seamless Escalation:** Intelligent hand-off to humans for high-empathy or high-risk situations.
