# Kitman Labs

Kitman Labs is a Dublin-headquartered company that provides an Intelligence Platform used by over 2,000 elite sports teams globally (including Premier League and NFL teams). Their AI helps coaches and medical staff optimize athlete performance and reduce injury risk.

## ⚽ Performance Intelligence
- **Injury Prediction:** Using machine learning to identify patterns that lead to injuries before they happen.
- **Load Management:** Optimizing training volume based on an athlete's physiological and psychological readiness.
- **Talent Identification:** Using data to find the next generation of elite performers.
