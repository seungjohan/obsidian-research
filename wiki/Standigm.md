# Standigm

Standigm is an AI-driven drug discovery company that automates the transition from target identification to lead optimization.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Biotech / Drug Discovery / Deep R&D.
- **Mission:** To industrialize the R&D process and eliminate guesswork in drug development.
- **Vision:** To become a "full-stack pharmaceutical company" powered by automation.
- **Status:** Series C Scale-up (Global offices in Seoul, Cambridge UK, Cambridge MA).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **ASK-LLM:** A RAG-based tool that transforms massive biological knowledge graphs into actionable drug targets.
- **STELLA & SPICA:** Commercialized internal tools into SaaS applications for molecular optimization and patentability prediction.
- **Pipeline Progression:** Advancing proprietary programs for Parkinson's, Autism, and NASH.
- **Closed-loop Workflow:** Moving toward an entirely autonomous "Target-to-Lead" workflow.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Funding:** $71M+ cumulative.
- **Speed Moat:** Discovery cycle reduced to 7 months (vs. years for traditional pharma).
- **Success Rate:** AI-discovered drugs are seeing 80-90% Phase I success rates (double traditional benchmarks).
- **Investors:** Includes Kakao Ventures, SoftBank Ventures Asia, and SK.
