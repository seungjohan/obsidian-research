# Nota AI

Nota AI is a global leader in Edge AI optimization, specializing in making high-performance AI models run efficiently on resource-constrained hardware.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Edge AI / Model Optimization / Physical AI.
- **Mission:** To bridge the gap between heavy AI models and edge hardware through "constraint-aware" optimization.
- **Vision:** "AI Everywhere"—democratizing AI for all hardware platforms.
- **Status:** Publicly Traded (KOSDAQ: Listed November 2025).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Samsung Partnership:** Supplies core optimization for **Exynos 2600**, enabling advanced on-device generative AI.
- **NetsPresso® Evolution:** Expanding the platform to support Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems.
- **Generative Safety:** Launched **Nota Vision Agent (NVA)**, a generative AI monitoring solution for proactive safety.
- **NVIDIA Collaboration:** Won the 2026 Nemotron Hackathon for data-centric Mixture of Experts (MoE) quantization.
