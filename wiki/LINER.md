# LINER

LINER (Aurum Planet) is a global leader in AI Search and Research, transitioning from a web-highlighter into a high-precision AI agent for knowledge workers.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Search / EdTech / Productivity.
- **Mission:** To empower knowledge workers with "Trustworthy AI" that eliminates misinformation.
- **Vision:** To revolutionize information discovery through high-precision, synthesized answers (Moving beyond "blue links").
- **Status:** Pre-Unicorn (Global leader in AI Search).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Global Dominance:** 90% of users are outside Korea, with 60% of paid subscribers in the US.
- **Agentic Shift:** Transitioning from a search engine to an **AI Research Agent** that can perform complex, multi-step tasks.
- **Academic Integration:** Partnered with top universities (e.g., Yonsei) to create AI-first campuses.
- **Hardware Integration:** Pre-installed on Lenovo PCs and integrated into SK Telecom's "A. Dot" platform.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **User Base:** 12 million+ global users.
- **Growth:** 341.3% YoY revenue increase.
- **Accuracy:** Scored 95.3 in OpenAI’s *SimpleQA* benchmark (outperforming GPT-4.5).
- **Funding:** ~$32M cumulative (Series C discussions ongoing).
- **Recognition:** Ranked #2 in Education on *Fast Company’s* 2026 Most Innovative Companies.
