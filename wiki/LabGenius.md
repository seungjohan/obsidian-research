# LabGenius

LabGenius is a drug discovery company that has built an autonomous "closed-loop" platform for the discovery of protein-based medicines. Located in Bermondsey, London, their facility combines synthetic biology, robotic automation, and machine learning.

## 🔬 Core Innovation
- **EVA:** Their autonomous robotic scientist that designs, conducts, and learns from its own experiments.
- **Active Learning:** Instead of training on static datasets, the AI proactively chooses which experiments will yield the most informative data.
- **Protein Engineering:** Specifically focused on complex multi-specific antibodies.
