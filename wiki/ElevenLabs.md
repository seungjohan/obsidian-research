# ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is a London-based AI company specializing in natural-sounding speech synthesis. Their platform allows users to generate high-quality audio in dozens of languages and even "clone" their own voice with just a few minutes of audio data.

## 🎙️ Audio Innovation
- **Text-to-Speech:** High emotional range and natural prosody.
- **Speech-to-Speech:** Changing the speaker of an existing audio track while maintaining the original performance.
- **Dubbing:** Automatically translating and re-voicing video content while keeping the original speaker's voice.
