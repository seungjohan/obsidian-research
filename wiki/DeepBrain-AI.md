# DeepBrain AI

DeepBrain AI is a pioneer in "Virtual Human" technology, specializing in high-fidelity AI avatars for interactive and generative video applications.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Video / Virtual Humans / Agentic AI.
- **Mission:** To humanize AI through high-fidelity digital twins and interactive avatars.
- **Vision:** To bridge the gap between humans and technology through "Conversational AI Humans."
- **Status:** Series B Scale-up (Unicorn Candidate).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Agentic Pivot:** Launched **B2B AI Video Agents**—avatars that can listen, understand, and respond in real-time for interactive operations.
- **Digital Mentors:** Positioning avatars as internal knowledge access points for large enterprises.
- **Mobile Expansion:** Focus on "Instant AI Video Creation" via the AI Studios mobile app.
- **Deepfake Security:** Introduced high-precision detection solutions to position as a "Responsible AI" provider.
- **User Experience:** Added 100+ "casual" avatars to move beyond the news-anchor aesthetic.
