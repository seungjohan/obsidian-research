# FuriosaAI

FuriosaAI is a leading AI semiconductor company specializing in high-performance, energy-efficient NPUs (Neural Processing Units) for data center inference.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Semiconductors / Data Center Infrastructure.
- **Mission:** To democratize high-performance AI inference through sustainable computing.
- **Vision:** To break the reliance on power-hungry GPUs by providing the most efficient inference engine.
- **Status:** Series D / Pre-IPO ($2.3B Valuation).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **RNGD (Renegade) Launch:** Mass production of the 2nd-gen RNGD chip (TSMC 5nm, HBM3). It delivers 2.25x better performance-per-watt than Nvidia A100.
- **Strategic Independence:** Rejected a Meta acquisition offer in 2025 to scale independently toward a 2027 IPO.
- **Global Expansion:** Opened a European flagship office in Lisbon, Portugal, and hired industry veterans to lead global infrastructure.
- **Developer Ecosystem:** Focusing on "PyTorch Eager Mode" support and speculative decoding features to lower the barrier for developers.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** ~$2.3B (₩3 Trillion).
- **Funding:** Raising a $500M Series D (Pre-IPO) led by Morgan Stanley.
- **Scale:** Target delivery of 20,000 NPUs in 2026.
- **Power Efficiency:** 180W TDP (allows deployment in standard air-cooled racks).
