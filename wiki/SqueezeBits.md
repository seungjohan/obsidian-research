# SqueezeBits

SqueezeBits is an AI model compression and optimization specialist that enables large-scale AI to run efficiently on diverse hardware, from cloud GPUs to edge NPUs.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Infrastructure / Model Optimization / Edge AI.
- **Mission:** To provide full-stack optimization that makes AI fast and cost-effective everywhere.
- **Vision:** To "Unlock the Potential of AI" by removing hardware and cost barriers.
- **Status:** Pre-Series A Scale-up (Backed by Samsung NEXT, Naver D2SF, Qualcomm).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **OwLite Toolkit:** A low-code platform for model quantization (32-bit to 4-bit) that maintains performance.
- **Physical AI Pivot:** Launching the **RoBoost Agent** to generate synthetic video for robotics 3.1x faster than standard pipelines.
- **Qualcomm Strategic Hub:** Official support for Qualcomm Neural Network (QNN) to enable high-speed on-device AI.
- **Cost Reduction:** Targeting 40-70% reduction in inference costs for enterprises.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Growth:** Transitioning from research-heavy to a revenue-generating infrastructure provider.
- **Scale:** Inference speed boosts of up to 3.1x for frontier models (NVIDIA Cosmos, Qwen).
- **Strategic Backing:** One of the few startups backed by both Samsung and Naver simultaneously.
- **Global Presence:** Participating in GTC 2026 to target the NPU enablement market.
