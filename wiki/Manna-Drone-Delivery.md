# Manna Drone Delivery

Manna is a Dublin-headquartered company that has built an aviation-grade drone delivery platform. Their drones are designed to fly at 80km/h and can deliver goods within a 3km radius in under 3 minutes.

## 🚁 Drone as a Service (DaaS)
- **High Frequency:** Designed for high-volume, short-distance deliveries (coffee, pharmacy, groceries).
- **Safety First:** Built-in fail-safes including parachutes and obstacle avoidance.
- **Operational Scalability:** Already operating in several Irish towns and expanding into Europe and the US.
