# Lunit

Lunit is a global leader in AI-driven medical imaging and precision oncology, famous for its INSIGHT and SCOPE product lines.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Healthcare / Medical AI / Biotech.
- **Mission:** To conquer cancer through AI, providing high-precision diagnostics and biomarker analysis.
- **Vision:** To become the "standard of care" in medical diagnostics and oncology.
- **Status:** Publicly Traded (KOSDAQ: 328130).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Pivot to Profitability:** Shifting strategy toward achieving EBITDA break-even by late 2026, with a revenue target of KRW 95.1B.
- **Enterprise Ecosystems:** Moving beyond individual algorithms to "End-to-End Ecosystems" (e.g., Lexington Clinic deal) covering the full patient journey.
- **FDA Milestone:** Received clearance for **Lunit INSIGHT 3D Mammography AI v1.2** with temporal comparison capabilities.
- **Lunit SCOPE Surge:** Rapidly expanding its oncology division through partnerships with global "Big Pharma" for clinical trials.
- **Acquisition Integration:** Successfully integrated **Volpara Health**, solidifying its presence in the US market.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** Market cap of ~$800M - $900M USD (KRW 1.2 Trillion).
- **Revenue:** KRW 83.1B in 2025 (53% YoY growth); 92% from overseas sales.
- **Funding:** Raised KRW 250B ($185M) in early 2026 for debt elimination and growth.
- **Reach:** Global market leader in AI-based radiology and oncology diagnostics.
