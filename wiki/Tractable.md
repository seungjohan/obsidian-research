# Tractable

Tractable is a London-based AI unicorn that uses deep learning to automate visual tasks. Their primary application is in the insurance industry, where their AI can look at photos of a car accident and instantly estimate repair costs, allowing claims to be settled in minutes rather than weeks.

## 📸 Visual AI Applications
- **Auto Claims:** Assessing vehicle damage from smartphone photos.
- **Property Damage:** Using aerial imagery to assess damage from natural disasters.
- **Refurbishment:** Checking the condition of recycled electronics.
