# Numra

Numra is a Dublin-based startup that develops AI "coworkers" for finance and accounting teams. Their agents are designed to handle high-volume, complex workflows like accounts payable, accounts receivable, and complex bank reconciliations.

## 📊 Finance Automation
- **Reconciliation Engine:** Using AI to match invoices with payments and identify discrepancies.
- **Workflow Orchestration:** Managing the end-to-end process from document arrival to ERP entry.
- **Error Detection:** Proactively identifying anomalies that standard rule-based systems miss.
