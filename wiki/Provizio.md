# Provizio

Provizio is a Limerick and Dublin-based company focused on automotive safety. They have developed a 5D Perception® platform that combines long-range imaging radar and AI to see through obstacles and adverse weather conditions, aiming to eliminate road accidents.

## 🚗 Perception Platform
- **Beyond Lidar:** Using radar-based imaging which is more reliable in poor weather than cameras or lidar.
- **On-Device Processing:** AI runs directly on the vehicle's edge for sub-millisecond reaction times.
- **Software-Defined Safety:** Continuous OTA updates to improve the "driver" model over time.
