# Limbic

Limbic is a London-based health-tech company that has developed *Limbic Access*, an AI-powered triage chatbot used by NHS mental health services. It helps patients access therapy faster while providing clinicians with high-quality diagnostic data.

## 🧠 Mental Health AI
- **Clinical Validation:** One of the first AI mental health tools to receive Class IIa UKCA medical device certification.
- **Improved Triage:** Reducing the time spent on administrative assessments by 40%.
- **Patient Engagement:** Providing a non-judgmental entry point for people seeking help.
