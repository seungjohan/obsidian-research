# Edge AI & Infrastructure (2026)

Edge AI in 2026 is moving LLMs and VLMs out of the data center and directly onto robots, cars, and wearables.

## ⚡ The Infrastructure Stack
- **Next-Gen NPUs:** Specialized chips from [[Rebellions]], [[FuriosaAI]], and [[DeepX]] that outperform GPUs in power-per-watt.
- **Model Compression:** Tools like [[SqueezeBits]]' OwLite that quantize models down to 4-bit without losing intelligence.
- **Hardware-Aware Software:** Platforms like [[Nota-AI]]'s NetsPresso that automate the matching of models to specific chips (Samsung Exynos, etc.).

## 🤖 Physical AI Applications
- **Autonomous Systems:** [[AIMMO]]'s DataOps and [[DeepX]]'s NPUs enabling Level 4 autonomy.
- **Industrial Vision:** [[MakinaRocks]] and [[Superb-AI]] deploying "Zero-Shot" models to factory floors.
