# Scatter Lab

Scatter Lab is an AI-native entertainment company famous for its social AI characters (Lee Lu-da) and the community-driven storytelling platform, **Zeta**.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Entertainment / Social AI / Consumer Apps.
- **Mission:** To alleviate human loneliness through emotionally intelligent AI companions.
- **Vision:** To make AI a "true companion" in human storytelling.
- **Status:** Profitable Scale-up (6+ consecutive quarters of profitability).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Zeta Ecosystem:** Shifted focus to a platform model where users create and interact with millions of AI characters.
- **Global Strategy:** Aggressive expansion into Japan and Southeast Asia.
- **Monetization:** Launched "Zeta Pass" and "Pieces" (in-app currency) for image generation and high-end model access (Koji/Luca).
- **Privacy Leadership:** Adopted "Privacy by Design" standards following past legal settlements, turning ethics into a brand moat.
- **Lorebook & Style:** Introduced advanced world-building tools for creators to fine-tune AI personalities.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Profitability:** Reached consistent profitability (rare for B2C GenAI).
- **Model Efficiency:** Vertically integrated stack reduces inference costs significantly.
- **User Growth:** Millions of user-generated characters; high Net Revenue Retention (NRR).
- **Tech Stack:** Proprietary LLMs (Luda Gen, Koji, Luca).
