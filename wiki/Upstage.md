# Upstage

Upstage is South Korea's leading generative AI unicorn, focusing on delivering "practical" AI that automates complex, document-heavy workflows for enterprises.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Enterprise AI / Business Productivity Software.
- **Mission:** "Building intelligence for the future of work."
- **Vision:** To become the world's #1 provider of practical AI that delivers real-world results in professional environments.
- **Status:** Unicorn ($1.1B - $1.2B valuation).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Agentic AI Leadership:** Launched **Solar Pro 3** (102B parameters), optimized specifically for multi-step task execution and deep reasoning (SWE-Bench/GPQA-Diamond top performance).
- **Sovereign AI:** Leading the South Korean national AI foundation model project to ensure data sovereignty.
- **Global Expansion:** Established hubs in San Jose (USA) and Tokyo (Japan). Their "Syn Pro" model is certified as a domestic foundation model in Japan.
- **Infrastructure Diversification:** Partnered with AMD to deploy Instinct MI355 GPUs, reducing reliance on Nvidia.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** ~$1.1B - $1.2B (Unicorn status achieved April 2026).
- **Revenue:** Triple-digit YoY growth (>130%), revenue tripled in 2024-2025.
- **Funding:** Cumulative investment of ~$285M (including $125.9M Series C).
- **Talent:** ~230 employees, including alumni from Apple, Amazon, and Nvidia.
- **IPO Status:** Targeted KOSPI listing in late 2026 / early 2027.
