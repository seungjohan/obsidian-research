# Protex AI

Protex AI is a Dublin-based startup that helps large enterprises in logistics and manufacturing improve their workplace safety. Their platform plugs into existing CCTV systems and uses AI to identify safety breaches (e.g., people walking in forklift zones, missing PPE).

## 🛡️ Workplace Safety
- **Proactive Alerts:** Identifying near-misses to prevent future accidents.
- **EHS Integration:** Providing Environmental, Health, and Safety teams with data-driven insights.
- **Privacy First:** Designed to protect worker anonymity while flagging safety risks.
