# Allganize

Allganize is an Enterprise AI platform specialist that provides "All-in-One" generative AI solutions, including specialized sLLMs and autonomous AI agents for knowledge workers.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Enterprise AI / SaaS / Productivity.
- **Mission:** To improve the lives of knowledge workers through AI automation.
- **Vision:** To make AI as accessible and diverse as smartphone apps for businesses.
- **Status:** Series B Scale-up (Global presence in US, Japan, Korea).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Alli AI Agents:** Pivoted to autonomous agents that act as "custodians" of specific business domains (HR, Legal, IT).
- **Zero-Labor Automation:** Focusing on tools that generate research-grade reports by connecting internal and public data.
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** Integrated MCP into their No-Code Agent Builder for seamless multi-tool orchestration.
- **Security-First Deployment:** Specializing in on-premise and private cloud LLM implementations for data-sensitive sectors (Energy, Finance).

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Customer Base:** 300+ enterprise clients including Mitsubishi Chemical and Nomura Securities.
- **Growth:** Ranked #1 in Japan for low-code/no-code AI app platforms.
- **Funding:** ~$47M cumulative.
- **Impact:** Achieved over 75% automation rates for major client service desks.
