# Quantexa

Quantexa is a London-based unicorn that specializes in Decision Intelligence. Their platform uses AI to connect massive, siloed datasets to create a single, 360-degree view of entities (people, organizations, transactions), allowing banks and governments to detect financial crime and manage risk.

## 🧠 Decision Intelligence
- **Contextual Data:** Moving beyond simple data analysis to "contextual" understanding of relationships.
- **Scalability:** Capable of processing billions of records in real-time.
- **Wide Adoption:** Used by major global banks like HSBC and Standard Chartered.
