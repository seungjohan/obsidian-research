# MarqVision

MarqVision is an "IP Operating System" that uses AI to protect brand integrity and recover revenue from counterfeits, piracy, and impersonation.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** LegalTech / SaaS / Brand Integrity.
- **Mission:** To protect and build a future shaped by original ideas by democratizing legal expertise and AI.
- **Vision:** To transition brand protection from reactive takedowns to proactive, intelligent brand control.
- **Status:** Series B Scale-up (Unicorn Candidate).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **From Defense to Growth:** Positioning IP protection as "revenue recovery," with 60% of users now in marketing/e-commerce.
- **Brand Integrity OS:** Expanded beyond counterfeits to anti-piracy, anti-impersonation, and unauthorized sales management.
- **Marq AI:** A generative AI suite that detects and enforces threats in as little as 15 minutes.
- **Global Expansion:** Operates in 118+ countries with offices in LA, Paris, Seoul, and Tokyo.
- **Network Dismantling:** Using AI to identify and dismantle entire rogue seller networks rather than individual listings.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue:** Projected $85M ARR by end of 2026 (Surpassed $20M in 2025).
- **Funding:** ~$94M cumulative (including a $48M Series B).
- **Customer Base:** 350+ global enterprise clients (Luxury, Fashion, Gaming, etc.).
- **Impact:** Typically drives 5-10% top-line growth for brands through revenue recovery.
