# PhysicsX

PhysicsX is a London-based startup founded by former Formula 1 engineers and theoretical physicists. They develop AI models that can simulate complex physics (fluid dynamics, aerodynamics, stress analysis) orders of magnitude faster than traditional methods.

## 🏎️ Impact Areas
- **Advanced Manufacturing:** Optimizing production processes in real-time.
- **Aerospace & Automotive:** Reducing the time to design more efficient, sustainable vehicles.
- **Renewable Energy:** Enhancing the performance of wind turbines and other energy infrastructure.
