# Ubotica

Ubotica is a Dublin-based company that is a global leader in "Orbital AI." They provide the hardware and software required to run AI models on satellites, allowing for real-time analysis of satellite imagery directly in space.

## 🛰️ Orbital AI
- **CogniSAT:** Their flagship platform that enables satellites to identify objects, detect changes, and compress data autonomously.
- **Energy Efficiency:** Running high-performance AI on the extremely limited power budget of a satellite.
- **Real-Time Response:** Essential for disaster response, maritime security, and environmental monitoring.
