# Nory

Nory is a Dublin-based startup that provides an AI-powered management platform for restaurant chains. It uses historical data and external factors (like weather and events) to predict demand, which then drives automated labor scheduling and food ordering.

## 🍽️ Restaurant Intelligence
- **Demand Forecasting:** Predicting exactly how many burgers or salads will be sold on a given Tuesday.
- **Labor Optimization:** Generating staff rotas that match predicted demand, reducing overstaffing.
- **Waste Reduction:** Optimizing inventory orders to ensure freshness while minimizing food waste.
