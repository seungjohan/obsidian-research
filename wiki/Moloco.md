# Moloco

Moloco (Machine Learning Company) is a global leader in AI-native ad infrastructure, specializing in operational machine learning for the digital economy.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AdTech / Machine Learning / Performance Marketing.
- **Mission:** To democratize access to advanced ML historically reserved for tech giants.
- **Vision:** To empower businesses through "operational machine learning" that turns data into measurable outcomes.
- **Status:** Unicorn (Profitable for 5+ consecutive years).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Performance CTV:** Bringing performance-based metrics (installs, direct outcomes) to Connected TV advertising.
- **Commerce Media (MCM):** Helping retailers build their own ad businesses (Retail Media Networks).
- **Streaming Monetization:** Launching AI-native ad stacks for major global streaming providers.
- **Privacy Resilience:** Models optimized for first-party and contextual data, thriving in the post-cookie era.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** $1.5B+ (Last official Unicorn round; secondary premium likely).
- **Revenue:** Estimated $200M+ ARR with high profitability.
- **Reach:** Reaches 2 billion daily active users across 3 million+ apps.
- **Scale:** Processes millions of bid requests per second with sub-second latency.
