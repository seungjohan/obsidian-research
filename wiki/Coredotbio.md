# Coredotbio

Coredotbio is a TechBio company that leverages equivariant AI foundation models to design mRNA therapeutics and optimize clinical trial success.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** TechBio / mRNA Therapeutics / Deep Science.
- **Mission:** To solve the high failure rate in drug development through an AI Co-Scientist platform.
- **Vision:** To build a "World Model" for biological systems to predict clinical outcomes.
- **Status:** Deep Science Scale-up (Focusing on the $50B+ mRNA market).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Helixir AI:** Launched a self-serve mRNA design tool for external research teams to democratize high-end computational biology.
- **AgentFabric™:** Deploying AI agents that "debate" drug protocols to optimize safety and efficacy before lab testing.
- **Organ-Targeted Delivery:** Focusing on extrahepatic delivery (brain, lungs) using AI-designed lipid nanoparticles (LNPs).
- **Synthetic Patient Matching:** Using AI to reduce clinical trial timelines by 30-50% through optimized patient matching.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Market Scope:** Targeting oncology and rare diseases beyond the vaccine market.
- **Efficiency Moat:** Significant reduction in "Cycle Time" from digital design to wet-lab prototype.
- **Strategic Focus:** Measured by "Partner Yield" (how much success they bring to Big Pharma partners).
- **Innovation:** Utilizing Equivariant Foundation Models (NeurIPS 2025 standard).
