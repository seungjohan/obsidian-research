# Nuritas

Nuritas is a Dublin-based biotech company that uses its AI platform, *Nuri*, to discover bioactive peptides from plant and food sources. These peptides can be used to improve human health through functional foods, supplements, and new medicines.

## 🧬 AI-Driven Discovery
- **Peptide Identification:** Predicting the health benefits of millions of different peptide sequences.
- **Clinical Validation:** Moving from AI prediction to lab and clinical testing to prove efficacy.
- **Strategic Partnerships:** Collaborating with global giants like Nestle and Mars to bring AI-discovered ingredients to market.
