# Riiid (Socra AI)

Riiid, recently rebranded as Socra AI, is a global leader in AI-driven education, famous for its high-accuracy score prediction and personalized learning engines.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** EdTech / Personalized Learning / AIaaS.
- **Mission:** To move beyond delivering answers and instead foster critical thinking through "Human-Growing AI."
- **Vision:** Providing every learner with a personal "AI Socrates" to guide independent learning.
- **Status:** Unicorn ($240M+ cumulative funding; SoftBank backed).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Socra AI Rebrand:** Pivoted from static tutoring to the Socratic method using generative AI.
- **AX (AI Transformation):** Licensing its proprietary "R.Inside" engine to schools and corporations to transform their educational content.
- **Santa TOEFL:** Partnered with ETS to provide official score evaluation and practice content ahead of 2026 reforms.
- **Global Celebrity Strategy:** Hired G-Dragon as a brand ambassador to scale public awareness.
- **J-Curve Growth:** Revenue exploded 15-fold in 2025 as the company hit a scalability inflection point.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue:** ₩47.1B in 2025 (1,432% YoY growth).
- **Scale:** Santa brand has 5M+ downloads across Korea, Japan, and Taiwan.
- **Accuracy:** Score prediction accuracy of 95% within 6-10 questions.
- **Funding:** $175M Series D from SoftBank Vision Fund 2.
