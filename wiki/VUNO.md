# VUNO

VUNO is a medical AI leader specializing in deep learning-based analysis of medical images, pathology, and bio-signals.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** Medical AI / Biosignals / Diagnostics.
- **Mission:** To improve patient outcomes through AI-powered clinical decision support.
- **Vision:** "View the Invisible, Know the Unknown."
- **Status:** Publicly Traded (KOSDAQ: 338220; Nearing full-year profitability).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **DeepCARS Dominance:** Their cardiac arrest prediction tool is deployed in 50,000+ beds, serving as the primary revenue engine.
- **U.S. Breakthrough:** Pursuing FDA approval for DeepCARS while scaling VUNO Med-DeepBrain in the US market.
- **European Expansion:** Partnered with Mesalvo and Contextflow to enter 900+ European hospitals.
- **Mayo Clinic Collaboration:** Developing AI-driven prognostic biomarkers for precision oncology.
- **HATIV B2C:** Expanding into consumer ECG monitoring and home-use diagnostics.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue:** ₩34.8B in 2025 (35% growth); ₩4.9B profit projected for 2026.
- **Market Cap:** ~$175M USD.
- **Market Share:** DeepCARS contributes ~74% of total revenue.
- **Financial Health:** Reached first-ever quarterly operating profit in late 2025.
