# Fetch.ai

Fetch.ai is a London-based lab that has developed a decentralized machine learning platform based on a distributed ledger. Their goal is to enable "Autonomous Economic Agents" to perform tasks like data sharing, trading, and coordination without human intervention.

## 🌐 The Open Network
- **Agent Discovery:** A protocol for agents to find each other and offer services.
- **Micro-Payments:** Integrated blockchain layer for frictionless agent-to-agent transactions.
- **AI Engine:** A system that translates human requirements into executable agent workflows.
