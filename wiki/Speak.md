# Speak

Speak is an AI-native language learning platform that provides a real-time, interactive tutoring experience using advanced speech recognition and LLMs.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** EdTech / Language Learning / Consumer AI.
- **Mission:** To help everyone reach conversational fluency in a new language.
- **Vision:** To build the most effective and accessible language tutor in the world.
- **Status:** Series B+ (OpenAI Startup Fund backed).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Agentic Tutoring:** Transitioned from basic chat to "Agentic Workflows" where the AI tutor manages entire curriculum paths and personalized learning agents.
- **OpenAI Integration:** Deeply integrated with the latest OpenAI models (GPT-5 series) for near-human latency and reasoning.
- **Global Expansion:** Rapidly scaling beyond Korea/Japan into Europe and Latin America.
- **Voice UX Moat:** Focusing on "Low-Latency Conversation" as the core product differentiator against general-purpose chatbots.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Growth:** One of the fastest-growing consumer AI apps globally.
- **Retention:** High daily active usage driven by the "AI Tutor" feature.
- **Funding:** Backed by OpenAI Startup Fund, Founders Fund, and Lachy Groom.
- **Scale:** Millions of users practicing speaking daily, moving away from passive "flashcard" learning.
