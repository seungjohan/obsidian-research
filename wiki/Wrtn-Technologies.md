# Wrtn Technologies (뤼튼테크놀로지스)

Wrtn is Korea's leading B2C AI portal, offering a platform where users can access various LLMs (GPT-4, Claude, etc.) and AI agents in one place.

## 🛠 Product Focus
- **AI Portal:** A unified interface for interacting with different AI models.
- **Wrtn Studio:** Tools for users to create their own AI agents without coding.
- **Content Generation:** Specialized tools for writing, marketing, and productivity.

## 🚀 Growth & Opportunity
- **Market Lead:** They have the largest B2C user base for AI in Korea.
- **Product Velocity:** Known for very fast shipping cycles and experimental features.
- **PM Role:** Focuses on retention, monetization of AI features, and the "Agent Ecosystem" strategy.

## 💼 Current Openings (2024-2026)
- **AX (AI Transformation) Product Manager:** (Seoul) Leading B2B AI solution development (AICC, Voicebots).
- **AX Project Manager:** (Seoul) B2B client management and AI delivery.
- **Product Manager Intern:** Frequent openings for 2-month sprints focusing on data and user research.

## 🚀 Product Strategy
- **CIC (Company-in-Company) Structure:** Independent units for "Wrtn Platform," "Crack (Creative)," and "AX."
- **Data-Driven:** Heavy emphasis on Mixpanel/BigQuery for validation.
- **"Agentic UX":** Shifting from a chatbot to an ecosystem of autonomous agents.
