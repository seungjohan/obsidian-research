# AIMMO

AIMMO is a global AI data platform leader specializing in end-to-end Data Operations (DataOps) for the automotive, robotics, and smart city sectors.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** DataOps / Autonomous Driving / ADAS infrastructure.
- **Mission:** To fast-track the development of autonomous systems through high-precision automated data operations.
- **Vision:** To become the "Global No. 1 AI Data Platform" powering the entire AI lifecycle.
- **Status:** Global scale-up (UK, Germany, US hubs).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **AD-DaaS (Data as a Service):** Providing a full-cycle process for autonomous driving data, from collection to curation and evaluation.
- **Edge Case Discovery:** Specialized in identifying rare driving scenarios (Operational Design Domain - ODD) that are critical for Level 4/5 autonomy.
- **Sensor Fusion:** Mastered 4D Radar and LiDAR fusion to support sophisticated 2026 sensor suites.
- **AIMMO Core:** An automated "Data Loop" platform that identifies edge cases and triggers refinement without human intervention.
- **Global Alliances:** Deep integrations with Tier 1 suppliers like Bosch and Continental.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Efficiency:** Delivers training data up to 10x faster than manual methods via smart automation.
- **Growth:** Massive revenue shift toward European and North American OEMs.
- **Reach:** Specialized in Level 4/5 autonomy requirements.
- **Investors:** Includes major VC backing for global expansion.
