# MediWhale

MediWhale is a "Reti-Intelligence" company that uses AI and retinal imaging to predict cardiovascular, kidney, and metabolic disease risks non-invasively.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Diagnostics / Cardiovascular / Ophthalmology.
- **Mission:** To provide radiation-free, non-invasive detection of life-threatening diseases.
- **Vision:** To make the eye the primary biomarker for systemic health globally.
- **Status:** Series C / Pre-IPO (Targeting 2027 IPO).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **FDA De Novo:** Securing US FDA approval for **Dr. Noon CVD** (formerly Reti-CVD) to enter the primary care market.
- **Revenio Partnership:** Integrated into iCare's global fundus imaging hardware.
- **Dr. Noon CKD:** Launching AI for chronic kidney disease risk prediction.
- **Domestic Scaling:** Partnered with Dong-A ST to distribute across internal medicine clinics, targeting the "Diabetes/Hypertension" patient base.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue Growth:** Target of 3-4x growth in 2026 (₩6-8B).
- **Adoption:** Surpassed 170 medical institutions in 15 countries.
- **Clinical Proof:** 40,000+ examinations conducted with high accuracy vs. CT scans.
- **Funding:** Recently raised ₩15B ($11M) Series C/Pre-IPO round in March 2026.
