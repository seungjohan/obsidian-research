# Rebellions

Rebellions is South Korea's premier AI semiconductor company, positioned as a full-stack AI infrastructure provider following its merger with Sapeon Korea.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Semiconductors / Data Center Infrastructure.
- **Mission:** To bridge the gap between AI training and real-world deployment by solving the "inference bottleneck."
- **Vision:** To become a global leader in AI inference infrastructure (Full-stack provider).
- **Status:** Unicorn (South Korea's first AI chip unicorn).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Sapeon Fusion:** Successfully integrated Sapeon Korea, leveraging SK Group's ecosystem (SK Hynix for HBM, SK Telecom for validation).
- **Product Launch:** Unveiled **RebelRack** and **RebelPOD** inference units for enterprise data centers.
- **Technical Milestone:** Demonstrated **REBEL-Quad**, a 4-chiplet design using HBM3E, matching GPU performance with superior cost efficiency.
- **Telecom AI Alliance:** Formed a strategic alliance with Arm and SK Telecom to build energy-efficient AI infrastructure.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** ~$2.34 Billion (3.4 Trillion KRW).
- **Funding:** $850M cumulative (including a $400M Pre-IPO round in March 2026).
- **Growth:** 10x revenue growth in 2025.
- **Talent:** 300+ employees, planning to double in 2026.
- **Investors:** Saudi Aramco, Samsung, SK Hynix, Arm, Mirae Asset.
