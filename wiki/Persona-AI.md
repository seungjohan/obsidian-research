# Persona AI

Persona AI (페르소나AI) is an Enterprise AX (AI Transformation) specialist focusing on AICC and on-device Generative AI solutions.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AICC / Enterprise AI / Physical AI.
- **Mission:** To lead the "Intelligent Transformation" with secure, lightweight, and cost-effective AI.
- **Vision:** "AI for Everyone" (Accessible AI that doesn't require massive infrastructure).
- **Status:** Preliminary Unicorn (SK Telecom is the 3rd largest shareholder).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **CES 2026 Success:** Won two Innovation Awards for **Mentor Lens** (Smart Glasses) and **AI Tutor** (On-device assistant).
- **On-Device AX:** Partnering with PC manufacturers (Sambo, ATEC) to launch AI PCs that run proprietary sLLMs locally.
- **SK Telecom Synergy:** Integrated into SKT's global AICC platform and "AI Persona" services.
- **Execution-type AI:** Moving beyond chatbots to agents that can handle reservations, billing, and complex tasks autonomously.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue Target:** Targeting the KRW 450B AICC market in Korea.
- **Ownership:** Strategic backing from SK Telecom provides a massive enterprise sales channel.
- **IP Portfolio:** 9+ proprietary products including **KGPT** (Enterprise GPT) and **SONA** (Dialogue engine).
- **Recognition:** Named "Preliminary Unicorn" by the Korean government.
