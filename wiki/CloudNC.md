# CloudNC

CloudNC is a London-based company focusing on the automation of CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machining. Their core technology, CAMAssist, uses AI to automatically generate the toolpaths required to machine a part, a task that traditionally requires hours of expert human programming.

## 🛠️ Product Strategy
- **Autonomous Manufacturing:** Moving from human-assisted CAD/CAM to autonomous production.
- **Democratizing Precision:** Enabling less experienced operators to produce high-precision parts.
- **Global Scaling:** Partnering with major CAD providers (like Autodesk) to embed AI directly into the design workflow.
