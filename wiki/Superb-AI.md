# Superb AI

Superb AI is an "All-in-One Vision AI" platform provider that automates the MLOps lifecycle for industrial and high-stakes sectors.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** MLOps / Vision AI / Physical AI.
- **Mission:** To democratize industrial vision AI by reducing deployment cycles from months to days.
- **Vision:** To become the foundational operating layer for AI in the physical world.
- **Status:** Pre-IPO ($43M+ total funding; IPO planned for late 2026).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Superb ZERO:** Launched Korea's first industrial vision foundation model, allowing deployment via natural language prompts (Zero-shot).
- **NVIDIA Physical AI Ecosystem:** Key partner for industrial Video Surveillance Systems (VSS) and edge computing.
- **Agentic MLOps:** Integrated AI agents for automated data triage, quality assurance, and model retraining.
- **Compliance Moat:** Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification, critical for 2026 defense and enterprise contracts.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **IPO Roadmap:** Tapped Hanwha Asset Management for a 2026 public listing.
- **Customer Base:** 100+ enterprise clients including Samsung, Hyundai, Toyota, and LG.
- **Time-to-Value:** Reduced AI training cycles from 9 weeks to near-instant deployment.
- **Funding:** Recent $9.5M Pre-IPO round (Dec 2025).
