# DeepX

DeepX is a high-performance AI semiconductor company focusing on NPU (Neural Processing Unit) technology for Edge and Physical AI applications.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** AI Semiconductors / Edge AI / Robotics.
- **Mission:** To decouple AI from the cost and energy limitations of the cloud.
- **Vision:** To become the "Arm of the AI industry" for Physical AI infrastructure.
- **Status:** Series C Scale-up (Global purchase orders across 8 countries).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Physical AI Alliance:** Partnering with Baidu and Ultralytics to unify edge AI software and hardware ecosystems.
- **Hyundai Robotics Lab:** Deploying on-device AI in robots and security systems for real-time autonomy.
- **On-Device LLM:** Their DX-M2 chip enables 100B parameter models to run locally at the edge.
- **2nm Roadmap:** Unveiled a roadmap for 2nm process semiconductors to maintain a lead in power efficiency.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Revenue Target:** ₩60 Billion ($40M) for 2026 (18x growth).
- **Customer Pipeline:** ~350 global companies in Proof-of-Concept (PoC) phase.
- **Manufacturing:** Achieved a world-class 91.1% yield rate on Samsung 5nm process.
- **Market Presence:** Distributed globally through Avnet, DigiKey, and WPG.
