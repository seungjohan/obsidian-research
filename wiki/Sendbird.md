# Sendbird

Sendbird is a global leader in in-app communications, currently transforming into an AI-first Customer Experience (CX) platform.

## 🏢 Company Profile
- **Industry:** CPaaS / Conversational AI / SaaS.
- **Mission:** To build the "AI workforce of tomorrow."
- **Vision:** Transforming customer communication from a cost center into a value driver through autonomous AI agents.
- **Status:** Unicorn ($1.1B Valuation).

## 🚀 Current Moves (2026)
- **Delight.ai Pivot:** Rebranded core AI initiatives to focus on outcomes and "delight" rather than just message delivery.
- **Voice AI Breakthrough:** Launched sub-second latency Voice AI capable of handling "messy" real-world audio.
- **No-Code AI Builder:** Introduced "Actionbooks," allowing non-technical teams to orchestrate complex agent workflows.
- **Omnipresent Agents:** Context-aware agents that maintain a single conversation across SMS, web, app, and social media.

## 📊 Business Metrics
- **Valuation:** ~$1.1B (Unicorn).
- **Scale:** Powers 7 billion messages monthly for 300 million MAU.
- **Customer Base:** 4,000+ brands including DoorDash, Match Group, and Rakuten.
- **Funding:** $220M cumulative (SoftBank, ICONIQ, Y Combinator).
